Rails 4.1.9 and 4.0.13 have been released!

rafaelfranca, January 6, 2015

I am happy to announce that Rails 4.1.9 and 4.0.13 have been released. This will be also the last release of 4.0 series as announced in a previous blog post.

As per our maintenance policy, the release of Rails 4.2.0 means bug fixes will only apply to 4-2-stable, regular security issues to 4.2.x, 4.1.x, and severe security issues to 4.2.x, 4.1.x, and 3.2.x. In addition to these already stated commitments, I agreed to also apply bug fixes to 4-1-stable until the Rails 5 release. As before, we will announce in a future blog post when we will drop bug fixes support for Rails 4.1.
